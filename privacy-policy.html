Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Pixel to Print is designed with privacy as a priority. We believe in transparent, minimal data practices. This page explains exactly what happens when you use our tool.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your image dimensions and calculations never leave your browser.

100% Client-Side

All conversions and calculations happen directly in your web browser. No server-side processing is involved.

Local Storage

We use browser local storage solely to remember your theme preference (light/dark mode). You can clear this anytime via your browser settings.

No Tracking Cookies

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or any third-party tracking technologies on this website.

External Links

Our resource pages may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites. We encourage you to read the privacy policies of any external sites you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We recommend reviewing this page periodically.

Questions?
