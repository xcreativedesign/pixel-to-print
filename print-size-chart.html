How to read this chart

Each table shows minimum pixel requirements at two DPI levels:

300 DPI Photo prints, brochures, business cards, phone cases, metal panels ‚Äî anything viewed up close.
150 DPI Canvas wall art, polyester fabric, mouse pads, cushions ‚Äî surfaces with texture or viewed from distance.

If your image meets the 300 DPI column, it will print sharply on any medium. If it only meets the 150 DPI column, it is suitable for canvas and fabric but not for close-range photo prints. Use the Pixel to Print calculator to check your exact image against any specific size and medium.

Frequently asked questions

How many pixels do I need for a 4√ó6 photo print?

For a sharp 4√ó6 inch photo print at 300 DPI, you need a minimum of 1200√ó1800 pixels. At 150 DPI (suitable for fabric or canvas), a 4√ó6 requires 600√ó900 pixels. The 4√ó6 format perfectly matches the 3:2 aspect ratio of most DSLR and mirrorless cameras, so no cropping is needed when printing from full-frame or crop-sensor RAW files.

What pixel size do I need for an A4 print at 300 DPI?

An A4 sheet (210√ó297 mm / 8.27√ó11.69 inches) requires 2480√ó3508 pixels at 300 DPI. This is the standard for professional print-quality documents, brochures, and certificates. At 150 DPI, you need 1240√ó1754 pixels.

What is the minimum pixel size for a 16√ó20 canvas?

For a 16√ó20 inch canvas print at the industry standard of 150 DPI, you need a minimum of 2400√ó3000 pixels. This is the correct target ‚Äî not 300 DPI ‚Äî because canvas is an industry standard of 150 DPI, due to the fabric texture and typical viewing distance. At 100 DPI (minimum acceptable), you need 1600√ó2000 pixels.

What is the correct Instagram image size in 2026?

For Instagram feed posts in 2026: 1080√ó1080px (square 1:1), 1080√ó1350px (portrait 4:5 ‚Äî the highest-performing format), or 1080√ó566px (landscape 1.91:1). For Stories and Reels: 1080√ó1920px (9:16). Always upload at 1080px width minimum to prevent compression. Instagram's new tall grid (3:4) means portrait formats now show more prominently in profile grids as of early 2025.

Can I print a social media image? Will it be good quality?

It depends on the print size. A 1080√ó1080 Instagram image at 300 DPI only prints at 3.6√ó3.6 inches ‚Äî a small photo print. At 150 DPI for canvas, it prints at 7.2√ó7.2 inches. To print at common larger sizes (16√ó20, 24√ó36), you need AI upscaling. Tools like Topaz Photo AI or Magnific AI can upscale 4√ó with good results.
